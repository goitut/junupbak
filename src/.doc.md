# JUNUPBAK – Bakery Website Project Description

## Project Overview
JUNUPBAK is a modern, elegant, and highly functional bakery website designed to showcase our artisanal breads, pastries, cakes, and seasonal specials. The goal is to create a warm and inviting online presence that reflects the freshness, quality, and creativity of our baked goods while making it easy for customers to browse our menu, place orders, and connect with us.

---

## Objectives
- **Showcase Products Attractively** – High-quality photography, engaging product descriptions, and clear pricing.
- **Enable Easy Ordering** – A smooth online ordering process for both pickup and delivery.
- **Communicate Brand Story** – Share our journey, values, and what makes JUNUPBAK unique.
- **Engage Customers** – Through blog posts, seasonal promotions, and newsletter subscriptions.
- **Mobile-First Design** – Fully responsive layout for all devices.

---

## Target Audience
- Local customers looking for fresh bakery goods.
- Event planners and individuals seeking custom cakes or catering.
- Tourists or visitors searching for unique, locally made treats.
- Regular customers who want convenient online ordering.

---

## Key Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- Hero section with rotating carousel of featured products and promotions.
- Brief brand introduction with a **“Shop Now”** call-to-action.
- Quick links to menu categories (Breads, Pastries, Cakes, Specials).
- Customer testimonials for trust and credibility.

### 2. About Us
- The JUNUPBAK story — origin, inspiration, and passion for baking.
- Team introductions with friendly photos.
- Sustainable and ethical sourcing practices highlighted.

### 3. Menu / Product lists / Delivery orders / Specialities orders 
- Categorized product listing (Breads, Pastries, Cakes, Seasonal Specials).
- Each item has a photo, description, price, and **“Add to Cart”** button.
- Filters for dietary preferences (Vegan, Gluten-Free, Sugar-Free).

### 4. Online Ordering
- Step-by-step ordering interface.
- Options for delivery or store pickup.
- Secure payment integration (credit/debit cards, mobile money, Mpesa etc.).
- Estimated preparation & delivery times.

### 5. Custom Orders
- Form for customers to request custom cakes, catering orders, or bulk purchases.
- Option to upload inspiration images.
- Pricing estimate feature.

### 6. Blog & Recipes
- Monthly updates on seasonal offerings.
- Behind-the-scenes stories about our baking process.
- Simple recipes for customers to try at home.

### 7. Contact & Location
- Contact form with auto-reply confirmation.
- Store address with embedded Google Maps.
- Phone, email, and social media links.
- Operating hours.

### 8. Dashboard 
- Admin for admins to track activities of the bakery
- Users, users need to register in order to access contact us for any communication and also accessing menus for ordering their items

### 9. Riders 
- All riders need to rigister and appear on riders list for customers to easily pick one for delivery of their ordered items

---

## Design Style & Branding
- **Colors:** Warm tones (cream, gold, chocolate brown, pastel pink).
- **Typography:** Elegant serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body text.
- **Imagery:** Professional product photography with natural lighting.
- **Tone:** Friendly, inviting, and artisanal — a mix of modern minimalism with homely warmth.

---

## Special Features
- Newsletter subscription pop-up.
- Customer loyalty program section.
- **“Today’s Fresh Batch”** highlight on the homepage.
- Seasonal promotional banners (e.g., Valentine’s specials, Christmas treats).
- Social media feed integration (Instagram or Facebook).

---

## Expected Outcomes
By launching the JUNUPBAK website, we aim to:
- Increase customer engagement and online visibility.
- Boost sales through online ordering.
- Strengthen our brand identity as the go-to bakery for fresh, creative, and high-quality products.
